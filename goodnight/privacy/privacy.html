Privacy Policy

This app runs on Android and iOS platforms and uses the following permissions:

  • Android permissions:
    • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Notification permission)
    • INTERNET (Internet permission)
    • VIBRATE (Vibration permission)
    • FOREGROUND_SERVICE (Foreground service permission)
    • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK (Media playback foreground service permission)
  • iOS permissions:
    • UIBackgroundModes - audio (Background audio playback)

The app does not have any sign-up feature and operates solely on the client side without using any separate servers. Therefore, it does not collect personal information such as names or contact details directly.

However, the app automatically collects the following data through SDKs such as Firebase Analytics, Remote Config, Crashlytics, Google AdMob, and AppLovin:

  • App usage statistics and behavior analysis
  • Remote configuration data
  • App crash and error reports
  • Advertising delivery and performance data

The collected data is used to improve app services, enhance user experience, and optimize advertising.

Data processing may be outsourced to external providers such as Google and AppLovin who comply with relevant privacy laws.

Users can limit data collection by resetting advertising IDs or changing app permission settings.

For inquiries, please contact customer support within the app or by email.
